Step 6 of 6: Piece Finishing

  1. Use copper tongs and put your piece into a heated pickling solution for 10-20 minutes. Then take your piece out with copper tongs and clean with fine abrasive pads, and use filing tools to grind and smooth any unwanted pieces or sharp edges. Polish all areas using a series of radial bristle disk attachment on a rotary tool. You may also use polishing paper.
  2. Further polish using polishing compounds on a rotary polishing cone or buff.
  3. Finally, set your cabochon into your piece within the bezel wire using a bone tool to secure the bezel wire on the edges of the cabochon.
