Step 4 of 6: Silver Designing

  1. Start designing your pendant based on your cabochon’s unique shape, characteristics, and color patterns. I find that it’s best to start your designing using pencil and paper to allow for better creative freedom and quick modifications. Make sure to include appropriate space for bezel wire, which you will set you stone into at the end.
  2. Once you are happy with your design, use your sterling silver plates, wires, and other components to cut and shape pieces to match your design.
  3. For more information about sterling silver plates, wires, bezel wires, and other sterling silver component options, I recommend visiting Rio Grande.
