Welcome to this step-by-step tutorial on creating historic iron slag pendants!

In this guide, you’ll learn how to find and identify unique slag pieces, then transform them through lapidary techniques such as cutting, grinding, shaping, and polishing into striking cabochons. From there, we’ll cover designing a sterling silver setting design that complements your cabochon, teaching you how to fabricate, assemble, solder, fire, and finish the sterling silver setting. Then to put it all together, we’ll cover setting your cabochon into your finished setting. By the end, you’ll not only learn to craft a unique piece of wearable history but also appreciate the artistry of turning industrial remnants into beautiful jewelry.
